Unbroken

A stripped-down commitment tracker for one or more habits. Pick each rule, choose the number of days, and check in every day. Miss once and that chain breaks.

About

Unbroken is built around deliberate constraint. No dashboards, no social feed, no soft streak language. The product keeps attention on the actions that matter today.

How it works
01 Choose a habit or write your own rule.
02 Commit for 7, 14, or 21 days.
03 Track each active habit from the list.
