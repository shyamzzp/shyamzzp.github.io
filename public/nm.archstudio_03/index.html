The Architect

Dedicated to
the art of bungalows

Ar. Nikita Mistry is a Mumbai-based architect with over a decade of focused practice in residential bungalow design. Working at Irani Architects in Colaba — one of Mumbai's longest-standing practices — she has built a reputation for design that is both technically rigorous and deeply human.

Her specialization in bungalows is deliberate. She believes the bungalow is architecture at its most intimate: single-storeyed, grounded, human in scale. It demands thinking about space as it is experienced at eye level — how light enters a room at dawn, how a verandah frames a garden.

She is known for her uncommon dedication — clients consistently remark she listens before she draws, remains present through construction, and treats every home as if it were her own.

Areas of Mastery

What she brings to every project

01
Bungalow Design & Planning

Deep specialization in single-family typologies — from compact urban plots to expansive estates. Mastery in proportion, flow, and the relationship between indoor and outdoor living.

02
Site & Context Analysis

Every bungalow begins with the land. Thorough studies of solar orientation, wind, topography, views, and neighborhood context ensure each design grows naturally from its place.

03
Spatial Flow & Vastu

Thoughtful integration of Vastu principles with contemporary spatial planning to create homes that feel auspicious and deeply comfortable for the families who inhabit them.

04
Materials & Finishes

A refined sensibility for materials — natural stone, exposed brick, timber, handcrafted plaster — chosen for how they age, breathe, and respond to the local climate.

05
Interior–Architecture Integration

Built-in joinery, ceiling treatments, threshold details, and lighting designed as integral parts of the architectural concept — not surface decoration applied at the end.

06
End-to-End Project Management

From municipal approvals and structural coordination to contractor supervision — the full lifecycle managed with meticulous attention and genuine care for every detail.

How She Works

A process built on dedication

01
Listening First

Every project begins with an extended discovery conversation — understanding not just the brief but the life that will be lived inside. How does the family spend its mornings?

02
Reading the Land

Detailed on-site analysis: solar path, prevailing breezes, soil conditions, views worth framing. The design emerges from the site's own character, not imposed upon it.

03
Sketching the Vision

Hand sketches and massing studies exploring multiple spatial ideas. Clients are invited into this stage — it is a collaboration, not a presentation of finished decisions.

04
Detailing Every Corner

Working drawings, material specs, structural coordination, and municipal documentation — handled with the same care as the design itself. Nothing is left to chance.

05
Present on Site

She remains involved through construction — regular site visits, contractor briefings, quality checks. Design intent is protected at every stage through to final handover.

The Firm

Irani Architects

Irani Architects is a Mumbai-based practice of over 40 years standing — one of Colaba's most respected architectural firms. Founded by Mr. B.J. Irani, the firm has built its reputation on delivering intelligent, enduring architecture across residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional projects.

The firm's philosophy — understanding the client's world before designing their space — mirrors Ar. Nikita Mistry's own approach to bungalow work. It is this alignment of values that makes Irani Architects the natural home for her practice.

Based at 121, Kartan Bhavan, Colaba Causeway — a short distance from the city's most celebrated built landmarks and a fitting address for a firm of its stature.
