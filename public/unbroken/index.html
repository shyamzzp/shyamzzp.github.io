Unbroken

A stripped-down commitment tracker for one habit at a time. Pick the rule, choose the number of days, and check in every day. Miss once and the chain breaks.

About

Unbroken is built around deliberate constraint. No dashboards, no social feed, no soft streak language. The product keeps attention on the one action that matters today.

How it works
01 Choose one habit or write your own rule.
02 Commit for 7, 14, or 21 days.
03 Check in daily and keep the chain intact.
